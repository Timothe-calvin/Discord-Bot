About This Bot

Jokes & Facts Bot is an entertaining Discord bot designed to bring fun and knowledge to your server. It offers a wide variety of jokes from different categories, interesting facts, riddles, and more. Perfect for breaking the ice or learning something new, this bot helps create an engaging atmosphere in your community.

Bot Features

  • Multiple joke categories
  • Interesting facts
  • Food jokes and trivia
  • Chicken nugget facts
  • Rate limiting for spam protection
  • Usage statistics

Getting Started

Jokes & Facts Bot uses the . prefix for all commands. To get started, just type .help in any channel where the bot has access.

The bot has various categories of jokes and facts that you can explore using the commands listed below. For the best experience, try different categories to find what your server enjoys most!
