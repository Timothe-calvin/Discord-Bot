Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Discord Bots Hub ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information obtained from users of our Discord bots: How To Make, Space Bot, and Jokes & Facts (collectively "the Bots"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our Bots, we may collect and process the following data:

  • Server Information: Basic information about the Discord servers where our Bots are used, including server IDs, channel IDs, and user counts.
  • User Information: Basic information such as Discord user IDs and usernames when interacting with the Bots.
  • Command Usage: Information about how users interact with our Bots, including commands used, frequency, and timestamps.
  • Message Content: When necessary for bot functionality (e.g., when using the chat or weather commands), message content may be processed but is not stored permanently.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Bot services
  • To improve and optimize our Bots' features and user experience
  • To understand usage patterns and detect potential issues
  • To respond to user inquiries and support requests
  • To prevent abuse and ensure compliance with our Terms of Service
  • To generate anonymous, aggregated statistics about bot usage

4. Data Storage and Retention

We store the collected data on secure servers protected by industry-standard security measures. We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Usage statistics and logs may be retained for up to 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted or anonymized.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Bots may use third-party APIs and services to provide certain features. These include:

  • Weather information APIs (for the weather command)
  • Joke and fact APIs (for entertainment commands)
  • AI services (for chat functionality)

When using these features, certain data may be shared with these third-party providers. We only work with providers who maintain appropriate data protection standards.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytical purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify individual users.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our Discord server or website contact form.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and/or through Discord announcements.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our Discord server or website contact form.
